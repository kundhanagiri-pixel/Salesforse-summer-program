# Day 1: CRM Basics - Salesforce Summer Program

## 1. What is CRM
Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a system that helps companies manage all interactions with current and potential customers. It stores customer data, tracks communication, and streamlines sales and service processes to improve relationships and grow business.

## 2. Why companies use Salesforce
Companies use Salesforce because it is the #1 cloud-based CRM platform. It provides a 360-degree view of every customer, unifies sales, service, and marketing on one platform, and uses AI + data to help teams sell faster and deliver personalized experiences.

## 3. Key Salesforce Objects
- **Account**: A company or organization you do business with. Example: ABC University.
- **Contact**: A person who works at an Account. Example: Student John Doe.
- **Lead**: A potential customer who has shown interest but is not yet qualified. Example: High schooler who requested a brochure.
- **Opportunity**: A potential sale or deal being tracked. Example: John’s application for the Computer Science program.
